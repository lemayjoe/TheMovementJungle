# Sumo Chicken

## Cue
**Low the whole time. Cossack, feet together, switch.**

## Movement
Cossack squat side to side, passing through a feet-together squat in the middle. Stay low the entire time â€” never stand up between transitions.

## Pattern
1. Cossack squat (left leg bent, right leg extended)
2. Bring feet together into deep squat (stay low!)
3. Cossack squat (right leg bent, left leg extended)
4. Repeat

## Benefits
- Hip mobility
- Adductor flexibility
- Single-leg strength
- Ankle mobility
- Core stability
